Common questions
Do I need to do anything after the introduction?

No. Once you make the introduction, Estate Circle handles everything. We'll update you when the project closes and process your commission automatically.

Will this affect my relationship with my client?

Estate Circle operates discreetly and positions the introduction as a personal service you're providing your client. We never undercut your relationship — your client continues to see you as the trusted advisor who connected them with something exceptional.

What kinds of projects does Estate Circle handle?

Custom pools and spas, outdoor kitchens, home theaters, smart home systems, wine cellars, tennis courts, whole-home renovations, landscape design, guest houses, private docks, helicopter pads, and more.

What are the minimum project sizes?

We focus on projects of $50,000 and above, which naturally aligns with the estates we serve. There's no upper limit — we've coordinated projects well into seven figures.

Is this exclusive to Miami?

We're currently focused on Miami-Dade, Broward, and Palm Beach counties. If your clients have estates in those areas, we can work together regardless of where you're based.
